Full description:
This app adds Toronto TTC subways information to MonTransit.

This app provides the schedule as well as real time service alerts and news from www.ttc.ca, YouTube and @TTCnotices & @TTChelps on Twitter.

TTC subways serve Toronto in Ontario, Canada.

Once this application is installed, the MonTransit app will display subways information (schedule...).

This application only has a temporary icon and no screen: download the MonTransit app (free) in the "More ..." section bellow or by following this Google Play link http://goo.gl/pCk5mV

You can install this application on the SD card but it is not recommended.

The information comes from the GTFS file provided by the Toronto Transit Commission and the City of Toronto.
http://www1.toronto.ca/wps/portal/contentonly?vgnextoid=96f236899e02b210VgnVCM1000003dd60f89RCRD

This application is free and open-source:
https://github.com/mtransitapps/ca-toronto-ttc-subway-android

This app is not related with the Toronto Transit Commission and City of Toronto.

- Other: required for real-time service alerts and to read news from www.ttc.ca, YouTube and Twitter
